LE WAGON PROJECT

Flatify

Flatify is a mobile application that allows the user to upload his/her expenses and share them with the flatmates and keep track of them, to evenly split the payments and find peace at home.

WHAT

Mobile application

VISIT THE WEBSITE

http://flatify.club

GITHUB REPO

https://github.com/mauriarmora/flatify

FEATURE

Invite your friends

At the beginning of your journey, you'll be asked to invite new flatmates to your flat, in case you haven't one already. You'll be asked to introduce the flat's name, the total rent, and of course the email of your flatmates. If you already had one, you would be redirected to its dashboard.

FEATURE

Create and share your expensess

You can create new expenses with just a few clicks! First, choose the category: shopping, pets, utilities or others. Then, write the amount and a little bit of description. Once you choose the date and the flatmates you want to share the expense with (all by default) you also have the possibility to upload a picture of the bill.

FEATURE

Customize your flat

You can easily customize your flat uploading a picture of all your flatmates and you, for instance. You can also add new members to the flat, once again writing down their email and specifying the amount of rent. Finally, you can also remove flatmates or delete the flat.

FEATURE

Keep track of your own expenses

This app is all about sharing your expenses with your flatmates and finding peace at home, but you have to also find peace within you first. So you can also check out your own expenses and see if you have gone nuts this month. Take control of your expenses and see whether you are spending a lot of money or not, with a nice chart.
